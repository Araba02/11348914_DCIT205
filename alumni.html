Alumni Impact: Laptop Donations

We are thrilled to share a successful past project by the Alumni Association of Computer Science, where we donated 500 laptops to the Computer Science Departments. This impactful initiative, made possible by our dedicated alumni, reflects our commitment to empowering the next generation of tech leaders and enhancing the learning experience in computer science. A heartfelt thank you to our alumni for their generous support in making this project a reality.
